How to Play

In this enchanted forest, your decisions will shape your fate. Here's how to play:

  • Follow the story and click the Next button to progress through the dialogue.
  • When prompted, make a choice by clicking one of the option buttons. Each choice will lead you down a different path.
  • Your goal is to escape the forest. Some paths lead to a good ending, while others may lead to bad or neutral outcomes.
  • Pay attention to the descriptions and follow your instincts to make the best choices.
